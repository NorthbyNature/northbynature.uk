Why We Collect This Information

At North by Nature Exclusive, we are committed to building a vibrant and inclusive community that reflects a wide range of voices, backgrounds, and experiences.

As part of your application, we ask for your date of birth, gender, and ethnicity. This information allows us to:

  • Recommend relevant content and partnership opportunities tailored to you.
  • Support brands and businesses who are looking to work with specific demographics.
  • Champion diversity, equality, and equal representation across all opportunities.

Many of our partners — whether in fashion, entertainment, or events — may request individuals from particular demographics for campaigns or private opportunities. By better understanding who our members are, we ensure that all backgrounds are considered and celebrated fairly.

This data is collected purely to provide the best opportunities possible for you and will always remain confidential.
