Members

By becoming an Official Member of Exclusive by NBN, you'll be invited to our client private events, exclusive gatherings, and receive free, or discounted products and services from our partners. Additionally, you'll be entitled to VIP access or discounted entry to our own events.

How do I become a member?

Free memberships rely on our selection process which prioritises those from creative, business-oriented, or entertainment backgrounds. Other members can apply for membership through contacting us or by applying via our website.

Once you officially become a member you will be issued with a personal Membership Card that grants you all the privileges of becoming a member.
